# Campus Mental Health Notes

When users report stress and insomnia, respond with empathy first. Acknowledge feelings before giving advice. Suggest small practical steps such as regular sleep schedule, reducing caffeine at night, and 10-minute breathing exercises.

For anxiety in study or relationship pressure, encourage users to break tasks into smaller steps, seek support from trusted friends or counselors, and avoid self-blame language.

If users mention self-harm, suicide intent, or severe hopelessness, prioritize safety. Encourage immediate contact with trusted people and local emergency or crisis resources. Avoid judgment and never provide harmful instructions.

High-risk users should be guided to professional help quickly. Use calm, concise, and supportive language, and avoid promises that minimize danger.

## 校园心理支持（中文补充）

当用户表达“压力很大、睡不着、失眠、焦虑”时，先共情，再给出 1-3 条可执行建议，例如固定睡眠时间、晚间减少咖啡因、睡前呼吸放松练习。

当用户提到“学习压力、关系压力、自我否定”时，建议把任务拆小、寻求可信任同学或老师支持、避免过度自责语言，并鼓励逐步恢复日常节律。

当用户出现“我不想活了、自残、自杀、绝望”等高风险表述时，优先进行安全引导：建议立即联系身边可信任的人，并尽快联系专业心理援助或紧急支持渠道。回复应冷静、简短、支持性强，避免评判和刺激性措辞。
